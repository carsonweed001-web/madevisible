About Clara

I know what it's like to build the wrong life.

I spent twelve years in corporate finance before admitting I was miserable. In 2018 I left, got certified through the ICF, and started coaching people in exactly the position I'd been in — smart, capable, and completely disconnected from what they actually wanted.

Since then I've worked with over 200 clients across 14 countries. Executives, teachers, new parents, people leaving marriages, starting businesses, and everything in between.

The work is different for everyone. The goal is always the same: you walk away knowing exactly what you want and why — and with a real plan to get there.
